PANEER BUTTER MASALA

RECIPE=
take some oil in pain cut onions tomatoes, garlic, ginger and green chilly and add some cashewnuts and saute in oil. saute till the onion becomes golden brown make it cool and then grind in mixer and add tomatoes together and grind and make a smooth gravy. stir the mixer till it leaves oil. later add little water and boil the gravy till it become thick add paneer cubes to the gravy and garnish with coriander leaves.
